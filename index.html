FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Who can participate in the hackathon? expand_more

The hackathon is open to all university students, recent graduates, and professionals passionate about technology. You can be from any field of study.

How do I form a team? expand_more

You can register with a pre-formed team of up to 4 members. If you don't have a team, don't worry! We will have a team formation session and a dedicated Discord channel to help you find teammates.

Is there a registration fee? expand_more

No, participation in the IEEE Hackathon is completely free of charge. We want to make innovation accessible to everyone.

What should I bring? expand_more

Please bring your own laptop, chargers, and any other hardware you might need for your project. We will provide power, Wi-Fi, food, and plenty of coffee!
